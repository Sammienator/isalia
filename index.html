MOOD BOARDS

Mood boards are essentially a visual collection of the design elements you're considering for a room. They can range from collage-like images to an actual room mockup, depending on your design capabilities and visual preferences. You may even use both of these styles at different points in your planning process.

WALLPAPERS/ PANELS.

A wallpaper will always add depth to the space, as well as a special touch of originality, especially in the case of patterned wallpapers. We especially recommend flowery wallpapers or those inspired by exotic worlds.
